# Conflict Resolution Workshop (Half-Day)

## Goal
Make merge conflicts feel like a normal, solvable conversation — not a crisis.

## Agenda
- 30 min: anatomy of a conflict file
- 30 min: live conflict injection and resolution
- 30 min: three conflict stories (rename, delete, whitespace)
- 30 min: team conflict resolution race
- 30 min: wrapping up — tools, etiquette, recovery

## Activity: Conflict Injection
Instructor prepares two branches with opposite edits. Learners walk through resolving using `git status`, `git diff`, editor, `git add`, and `git commit`.

## Debrief Questions
- What made the conflict hard?
- What would have prevented it?
- How should teams communicate during conflicts?
