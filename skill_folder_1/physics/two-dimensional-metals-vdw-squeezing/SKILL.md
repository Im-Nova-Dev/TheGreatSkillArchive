# Two-Dimensional Metals via van der Waals Squeezing

Teach the 2025 Physics World Breakthrough of the Year: how to make atomically thin single-element metals (bismuth, tin, lead, indium, gallium) by squeezing molten metal between atomically flat MoS₂ anvils.
