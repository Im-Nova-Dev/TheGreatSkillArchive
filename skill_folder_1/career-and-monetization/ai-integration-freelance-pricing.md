# AI Integration Freelance Pricing

## 1. Market Benchmark (2026)
- **AI Integration Specialist median**: $120–200/hr (Jobbers Feb 2026)
- **AI Engineer U.S. salary equivalent**: ~$195k/yr
- **Security-augmented AI work**: commands +15–30% premium vs. vanilla integration

## 2. Engagement Tier Presets

| Tier | Rate (hr) | Typical Scope | Best Fit |
|------|-----------|---------------|----------|
| Audit / Discovery | $150–175/hr | 1–2 week readiness review | Mid-market + enterprise |
| Prototype / POC | $125–150/hr | 1–2 week vertical slice | SMB, startup |
| Production Build | $120–175/hr | 2–8 week RAG + integrations | Mid-market |
| Compliance-Wrapped Deploy | $140–200/hr | Adds audit, logging, RBAC | Regulated industries |
| Monthly Retainer | $3,500–7,500/mo | tuning, monitoring, iterations | Any with live systems |

Rules of thumb:
- Upcharge regulated industries (healthcare, legal, finance) +20–30%.
- Fixed-price only when boundary conditions are strict; time-and-materials for exploratory RAG work.
- Cap exposure: define integration count, document count, user count limits in SOW.

## 3. Offer Archetypes

### A. AI Readiness Audit
- Deliverable: scored report + 90-day roadmap
- Price: $8,000–20,000 fixed
- Timeline: 1–2 weeks
- Win condition: client has docs/data but no production AI.

### B. RAG Pipeline Package
- Deliverable: retrieval layer + evaluation harness + handoff runbook
- Price: $15,000–40,000 fixed OR $150/hr T&M
- Timeline: 2–6 weeks
- Win condition: existing docs/knowledge base; domain terminology matters.

### C. Compliance-Aware AI Copilot
- Deliverable: hosted AI + PII redaction + audit trail + role filtering
- Price: $20,000–60,000 first deploy + $2,500–5,000/mo retainers
- Timeline: 4–12 weeks first deploy
- Win condition: regulated workflows + auto-escalation for human review.

### D. Prompt Pipeline Engineering Retainer
- Deliverable: production prompt chains, evals, cost dashboards, weekly tuning
- Price: $3,500–7,500/mo
- Timeline: ongoing
- Win condition: high-volume AI ops with acceptable cost/quality KPIs.

## 4. Positioning Framework
Prioritize two axes:
1. **Domain depth** (industry language, compliance rules, internal tools)
2. **Integration depth** (RAG quality, cost optimization, observability, fallback behavior)

Lead with outcome, not tool. Sell:
- “reduced research time by X%” vs. “RAG with vector DB”
- “zero PII exposure in logs” vs. “audit logging added”

## 5. Negotiation Tactics
- Anchor high: propose audit at $12k–18k; discount to $9k–14k to land.
- Limit scope explicitly: 50 docs, 5 integrations, 10 users in trial; overages at agreed rate.
- Retain ownership of prompt assets and eval harness except written license.
- Require access to sandbox/test environment before fixed-price commitment.

## 6. Verification Checklist
- [ ] Rate within $20 of 2026 median for chosen tier
- [ ] Scope document names data/doc/user caps
- [ ] Two adjacent pricing models for same service (fixed vs. retainer)
- [ ] One compliance add-on when client is in regulated space
- [ ] Portfolio item proving exact capability being sold

## Failure Modes
- Pricing too low to filter tire-kickers; default to $120+ for time-based work.
- Selling tool knowledge rather than domain outcome; lose to generalist or cheaper vendor.
- Skipping sandbox access; absorb hidden environment costs.
- Ignoring compliance angle in regulated verticals; leave 20–40% value on table.
