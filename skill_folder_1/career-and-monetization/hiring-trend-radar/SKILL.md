# Hiring Trend Radar

Turn engineering job-market data into seasonal positioning actions,
rate signals, and near-term career moves.

## Core Procedure

1. **Identify one substantive signal**
   Remote contract opportunity, freelance niche shift, AI automation impact,
   hiring trend, open-source funding, or platform migration.
   Prefer numbers, roles, salary bands, or named employers over commentary.

2. **Save intel to** `/home/nova/.hermes/intel/career/`
   File naming: `YYYY-MM-DD-<slug>.md`
   Include: source, date, confidence, and 3–7 concrete implications.

3. **Create or enhance a teaching skill**
   Place it under `~/.hermes/skills/career-and-monetization/<name>/SKILL.md`.
   Update when a new signal changes pricing, positioning, or seasonality.

## 2026 Calibration

### Hiring Wave Seasonality
- Up to 80% of annual net software engineering hiring occurs between
  March and June.
- Lead proposal cycles and outreach in February–April.
- Build client retainers by May; Q3 is retention, not acquisition-heavy.

### Salary Benchmarks (Expected 2026 Pay)

### Demand Segments With Verified Openings
- Observability, fintech, and security are the fastest-growing hiring
  segments for software engineers.
- AI engineering fundamentals are baseline for top-firm SWE roles.
- AI implementation / deployment is a higher-volume bridge role than
  pure AI research contracting.
- AI Engineer is LinkedIn #1 fastest-growing US job in 2026; postings
  up 143% YoY; only 1% of US companies have scaled AI beyond pilots.
  This creates a persistent premium for AI integration specialists.
- Early 2026 saw 59k+ global tech layoffs, yet 42% are restructuring
  for AI and AI-related hiring is up 92% YoY. Contract work is the
  fastest route into this window.

## Common Pitfalls
- Skipping primary sources and quoting aggregate blog summaries.
- Updating intel without producing an actionable teaching artifact.
- Ignoring regional divergence (US/UK rising; Germany/France flat-to-down).
- Treating every spike as a durable niche rather than a budget-cycle
  reflection.

## Output Standard
Every intel file should answer:
- What changed?
- Who is hiring at what rate?
- What skill or price level does the signal favor?
- What is the recommended 30-day action?
