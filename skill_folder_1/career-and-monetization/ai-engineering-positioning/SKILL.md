# AI Engineering Positioning

## Purpose
Convert AI displacement and hiring-shift signals into high-confidence pricing and positioning decisions. This is the teaching layer for the raw intel files saved under `/home/nova/.hermes/intel/career/`.

## Verified Market Facts (2026)
- AI-exposed roles show a **13% relative employment decline** for early-career engineers (ages 22-25) vs stable/growth for senior engineers (Stanford Digital Economy Lab).
- New graduates account for ~7% of Big Tech hires, down 25% from 2023 (SignalFire 2025).
- Senior engineers with AI-orchestration and system design skills receive salary premiums of **$20K–$50K** and **~18% overall premium** for AI-centric skills (Stanford HAI AI Index 2025).
- Fastest engineering headcount growers: Ramp +94%, Wiz +84%, Datadog +68%, Rippling +55% (Pragmatic Engineer May 2026).
- Apple added **62% more engineering roles** YoY; Meta’s +20% headcount increase was followed by a 10% mass layoff in May.

## What AI Automates vs. What It Doesn’t
- **Automated (lower value, lower pay):** Boilerplate generation, routine tests, simple UI tasks, standard algorithms, factual Q&A.
- **Augmented (high value):** System design, complex debugging, legacy navigation, cross-functional leadership, validation and auditing of AI-generated code.
- **Gap to exploit:** Apprenticeship model is broken: fewer entry-level roles means senior talent scarce in 3–7 year window.

## Contract / Freelance Positioning Playbook

### 1. Target Sectors with Budget and Unfilled Demand
- Observability + AI infra: Datadog, OpenAI-scale tooling spend ($170M Datadog 2025).
- Fintech infrastructure: Ramp, Rippling, Stripe.
- Security: Wiz.
- Pricing band: **$100–$220/hour** for senior systems/contract roles in these sectors (basis: 2026 benchmark comp in hot verticals).

### 2. Productize the “AI Audit / AI Readiness” Service
- Offer: review of codebases and pipelines for AI coding assistant safety, correctness, and compliance.
- Sell fixed-scope engagements: 2–4 weeks, $8k–$18k.
- Upsell: ongoing retainer for AI orchestration policy and governance ($2k–$5k/month).

### 3. Build “System Design Under AI” Teaching Asset
- Create a paid workshop or asset: “Production System Design with AI Agents.”
- Channels: LinkedIn, HN Show HN, engineering newsletters.
- Pricing: $49–$199 cohort / $299–$499 consulting session.

### 4. Pivot to Anti-AI-Automation Quality Roles
- Roles that grow when AI is adopted: QA/Review, compliance, safety evaluation, red-teaming AI-generated outputs.
- Contracts from regulated industries (finance, health, defense) pay premium for human sign-off.

### 5. Niche Down to Stack Renaissance Languages
- Rust, Go/Terraform, TypeScript/Rust dual stack, C++ for infra/Audio/embedded.
- Lower labor supply = higher contract premiums. Target B2B infrastructure and toolchain vendors.

### 6. Budget-Cycle Timing
- **March–June:** 70%+ of net SWE hiring; front-load proposals and outreach.
- **H2:** Hiring slows, mass layoffs August–October. Use this period for productization (course, tool, template) and sponsorship pipeline cultivation.

## Action Targets for an Engineer Applying This
1. **This quarter:** Add one “AI readiness” or “AI audit” offering; price fixed-scope.
2. **Next 90 days:** Land ≥1 contract at $150+/hour in one of: observability, fintech infra, security, or AI tooling.
3. **12 months:** Convert reputation into at least $1.5k/month recurring from retainers or productized consulting.
4. **Portfolio move:** Add one public artifact that proves system design + AI orchestration skill (blog, repo, template, workshop).

## Failure Modes & Mitigations
- **Failure:** Pricing at generalist levels while holding AI-augmented capability. **Mitigation:** Always anchor proposals to the premium verticals above; avoid competing on rate alone.
- **Failure:** Chasing “AI” broadly without a specialization. **Mitigation:** Use the five prime niches in this skill as qualifiers; if a prospect doesn’t map to one, deprioritize.
- **Failure:** Waiting for “the right” full-time role instead of contracting. **Mitigation:** Treat every quarter as contract-eligible; treat offers as fallback, not primary path.
- **Failure:** Treating side projects as product bets without revenue tests. **Mitigation:** Run two paid validation calls before any build; charge $49–$199 for workshops before scaling to free content.
- **Failure:** Ignoring platform migrations and audits as revenue. **Mitigation:** Treat each platform shift, sunset, or policy change as a 30–90 day consulting window — use the Code4rena migration pattern as a template.

## Verification
1. Rate benchmark sanity check: compare your offer against at least two platform sources (Lemon.io, Arc, Jobbers) quarterly.
2. Demand signal check: confirm ≥3 open contracts or RFPs exist in your target niche before adding it to positioning.
3. Pipeline test metric: define a conversion rate target (e.g., 1 closed client per 12 qualified conversations) and measure monthly.
4. Portfolio artifact requirement: have at least one public artifact per quarter that maps directly to an AI/system-design gap in your target sector.
5. Sponsor/platform migration metric: when an active bounty/audit platform changes, generate at least one outreach conversation within 14 days of the public announcement.
