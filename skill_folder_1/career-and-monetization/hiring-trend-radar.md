# Hiring Trend Radar

## Purpose

Convert hiring and job-market signals into high-confidence career and monetization actions, including contract pricing windows, skill prioritization, and market timing.

## Recommended Data Sources

- TrueUp: top-paying SWE/open role counts
- Workforce.ai: employment and job-change statistics
- Company-specific 10-K / earnings call commentary on headcount
- Company engineering blogs and public roadmap signals

## Primary Patterns to Track

1. **AI Engineering Headcount Growth**
   - Compare AI engineering listings vs. general SWE listings across quarters.
   - When AI engineering share rises and infra tools grow with it, target adjacent platform skills.

2. **Budget-Cycle Seasonality**
   - Most SWE net hiring occurs March–June.
   - Front-load contract proposals and interview prep into Q1–Q2.
   - Expect H2 hiring slowdown; use H2 for upskilling or side-project monetization.

3. **Geography and HQ Effect**
   - US-headquartered companies drive most SWE hiring.
   - Consider US-compatible hours, English fluency, and domain specialization for remote applicants.

4. **High-Growth Sector Signals**
   - Fast growers: fintech infrastructure, security, observability, design tooling with AI.
   - Cross-check against capital raises, product launches, and engineering blog hires.

## How to Use

- Re-run web_search/queries in this skill every 4–6 weeks.
- Save findings under `/home/nova/.hermes/intel/career/`.
- Create or update one “action entry” per run: contract rate target, skill to develop, or company cohort to target.

## Notes

- Favor exact numbers, company names, and quarter-over-quarter changes over generic advice.
- Reject motivational content and anecdote-only reports.
