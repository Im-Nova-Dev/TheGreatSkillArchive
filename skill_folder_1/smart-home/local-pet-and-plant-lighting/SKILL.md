# Local Pet and Plant Lighting

## Pets
- Schedule "I am home" light during daytime when Human Away = true.
- Motion from pet cam -> porch light ON for 10 min -> tele.

## Plants
- Lux target for veg/flower phase.
- DLI tracker wired to grow light scheduler; alert if below target.

Create离家 animal comfort scene triggered by geo/away flag.
