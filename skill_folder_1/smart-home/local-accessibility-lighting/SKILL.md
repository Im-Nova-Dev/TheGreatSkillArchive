# Accessibility Lighting

## Patterns
- High contrast toggle: black/white or warm/cool for low vision.
- Audio feedback on state change via Piper TTS.
- Single switch: one button cycles full room brightness/off.
- Auto hall light floor level for walker awareness.

## Teaching exercise
Create movement-safe night path: kitchen-hall-bath only lights on low at night.
