# Installer Safety Audit for Smart Lighting

## Before job check
- [ ] PPE: glasses, non-slip shoes, head clearance checked in attic/loft.
- [ ] Breaker labeled in panel.
- [ ] Non-contact tester charged and functional.
- [ ] Old fixture bagged for bulb disposal.

## LOTO simplified
1. Turn off breaker.
2. Tag handle with "WORKING - DO NOT TOUCH."
3. Verify dead.
4. Work.
5. Remove tag, restore power.

## GFCI rule
Outdoor + bathroom always. Test monthly on installed device.

## Disposal
Fluorescent tubes and ballasts: hazardous. Do not trash.
LED bulbs: household trash OK in most areas, but recycling preferred.

## After job
- [ ] No exposed copper.
- [ ] No trip hazards on floor.
- [ ] Wires in box tucked, not against switch back.
- [ ] Tools not left in ceiling/attic.
- [ ] Old materials removed from site.

## Why this matters
Smart work is electrical work. The same safety culture applies whether installing a $12 bulb or a $500 pendant.
