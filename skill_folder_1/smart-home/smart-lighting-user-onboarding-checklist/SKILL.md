# User Onboarding Checklist: Lighting

## Before you leave
- [ ] Every device named as room + position.
- [ ] At least one scene per room.
- [ ] Voice command tested and shown twice.
- [ ] Physical remote or switch labeled and demonstrated.
- [ ] App icons placed on primary phone home screen.
- [ ] Household policy stated: smart switches stay ON; control by app/remote only.
- [ ] Troubleshooting card printed and left on counter with support contact.

## Household policy agreement
Agree on these rules together:
1. Leave physical wall switch ON unless house is vacant.
2. Do not install mixed smart+brand bulbs in same room without explaining.
3. Motion-triggered lights assume 60s timeout.

## Voice rehearsal phrases
- "Turn on <room> light."
- "Set <room> light to 50%."
- "Goodnight."
