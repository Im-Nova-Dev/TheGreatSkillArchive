# Smart Truck / Work Lights Installation Mastery

## Market reality check
Most "smart" vehicle lighting is limited to interior cabin RGB (not under-hood or truck-mounted work lights). Heavy-duty truck lights from Rigid, Grote, Baja Designs are not app-connected. Smart vehicle work lighting is still a niche/product gap in 2025-2026.

## What actually exists
  - Govee RGBIC Integrated LED Interior Car Lights: 12V, under-dash and door-install app-controlled strips.
  - Govee garage lights (100ft RGBIC) installed in garage bays, not vehicle-mounted permanently.
  - Universal smart LED bars with IR/remote: not truly network-connected.

## Installation: Govee interior car lights (12V)
1. Locate 12V accessory/cigarette-lighter circuit, or tap into fuse box.
2. Route USB or 12V wire along headliner/door seal; keep away from SRS airbag deployment zones.
3. Connect controller; use QR/manual add in Govee Home app.
4. Test effects before sticking strips: verify color zones function.

## Installation: garage bay lights
1. Choose IP20 for indoor garage; IP65 if door opens to rain exposure.
2. Mount with brackets into ceiling trusses or use adhesive on smooth surfaces.
3. Power injection at end of 100ft run to prevent dimming at tail.

## Heavy-duty truck lights (non-smart)
- Reference for non-smart install: Rigid Industries, Grote e Series, Baja Designs.
- Install with relay and switch in cab; fuse within 12in of battery.
- Wire gauge: 10AWG for long runs >6ft at high current.
- Ground to chassis; scrape paint to bare metal.

## Safety rules
- Do not install RGB strips inside engine bay; engine heat degrades silicone and adhesives within months.
- Route wires away from moving linkages (brake pedal, steering column linkage).
- Use fuse no larger than wire ampacity; 12V 10A = 120W max on 16AWG wire.

## Teaching cues
- Explain the difference between "app controlled" and "IR remote controlled" so buyers don't overpay expecting Matter-smart.
- Discuss when to use smart plug-controlled garage lights versus hardwired relay install.

## Post-install
- [ ] All lights operational in intended zones.
- [ ] Smart features confirmed (where applicable) by app pairing and effect test.
- [ ] Fuse rating verified; no hot wires touching chassis.
