# Teaching Scenes Without Overwhelming Users

## The metaphor
"Scene = saved mood." Every light setting together is a scene. Just as you bookmark a song station, you save one brightness/color combo.

## Four starter scenes every user needs
  1. Morning: brightness 80%, 4000K.
  2. Evening: brightness 50%, 2700K.
  3. Movie: brightness 15%, 2700K, strips blue.
  4. Off: all lights off, locks engage.

## Ownership progression
- Hour 1: user scenes from app.
- Day 1: add time-based automation.
- Week 1: add sensor trigger.

## Why voice/app distraction is normal
Users press app buttons 10 times before realizing voice exists. Expect this. Schedule a 10-minute voice practice session on Day 2.

## Post-setup coaching
- Provide printed card near kitchen with 3 canonical phrases.
- Remove unused apps from phone home screen to reduce confusion.
- Schedule 30-day "scene audit" to delete unused scenes.
