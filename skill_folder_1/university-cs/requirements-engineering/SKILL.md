# Requirements Engineering

## Core topics

1. Discovery
   - Stakeholders and interviews
   - Context and goals

2. Specification
   - Functional requirements
   - Nonfunctional requirements

3. Management
   - Traceability
   - Change control

## Teaching approach
- Capture requirements for one small system.
- Identify one ambiguous statement.
- Convert one requirement to testable condition.
