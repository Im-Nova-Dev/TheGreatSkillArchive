# Voting Theory

## Core topics

1. Rules
   - Plurality and ranked-choice
   - Approval and score voting

2. Criteria
   - Fairness properties
   - Condorcet and Arrow ideas

3. Design
   - Ballot design
   - Tactical incentives

## Teaching approach
- Compare voting rules on one example.
- Explain one paradox.
- Evaluate one organizational decision method.
