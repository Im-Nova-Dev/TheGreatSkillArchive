# Mentoring

## Core topics

1. Roles and relationship
   - Mentor and mentee expectations
   - Trust and safety

2. Skills
   - Goal setting
   - Feedback and questioning

3. Practice
   - Career guidance
   - Sponsorship

## Teaching approach
- Design one mentoring goal setting exercise.
- Roleplay one feedback conversation.
- Review one mentoring relationship for health.
