# Expert Systems

## Core topics

1. Knowledge
   - Rules and facts
   - Representation

2. Inference
   - Forward and backward
   - Certainty concepts

3. Practice
   - Shell concepts
   - Knowledge acquisition

## Teaching approach
- Design one small rule base.
- Compare forward and backward chaining.
- Critique one expert system design.
