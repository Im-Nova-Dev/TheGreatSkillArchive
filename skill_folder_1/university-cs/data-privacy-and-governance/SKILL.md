# Data Privacy and Governance

## Core topics

1. Privacy
   - Principles
   - Techniques

2. Governance
   - Catalog and lineage
   - Access control

3. Compliance
   - Regulations overview
   - Impact assessment

## Teaching approach
- Review one data flow for privacy risk.
- Apply one governance rule.
- Design one consent and retention approach.
