# Mathematical Foundations for CS

## Core topics

1. Discrete math
   - Sets and relations
   - Functions

2. Proof and logic
   - Induction concept
   - Direct and indirect proof

3. Probability and numbers
   - Counting basics
   - Probability intuition

## Teaching approach
- Prove one simple statement.
- Model one combinatorial scenario.
- Relate one concept to an algorithm design.
