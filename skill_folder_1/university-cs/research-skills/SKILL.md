# Research Skills

## Core topics

1. Discovery
   - Literature search
   - Paper selection

2. Methodology
   - Experimental design
   - Reproducibility

3. Communication
   - Reviews
   - Reporting

## Teaching approach
- Search one topic efficiently.
- Read one paper critically.
- Draft one short literature note.
