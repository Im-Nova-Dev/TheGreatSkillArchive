# Artificial Intelligence Fundamentals

## Core topics

1. Agents and environments
   - Rationality
   - Problem formulation

2. Search and reasoning
   - Uninformed and informed search
   - Logical reasoning

3. Learning and knowledge
   - Concepts and representation
   - Interaction

## Teaching approach
- Model one simple agent.
- Search one small problem space.
- Discuss one rationality constraint.
