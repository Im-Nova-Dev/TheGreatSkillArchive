# Humane Technology

## Core topics

1. Ethics
   - Attention and agency
   - Manipulation risk

2. Design
   - Anti-dark patterns
   - Wellbeing support

3. Metrics
   - Wellbeing measures
   - Product outcomes

## Teaching approach
- Critique one interface for persuasive risk.
- Redesign one flow to respect attention.
- Propose one wellbeing metric.
