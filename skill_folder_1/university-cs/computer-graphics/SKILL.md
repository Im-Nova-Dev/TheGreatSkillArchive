# Computer Graphics

## Core topics

1. Geometry
   - Coordinate spaces and transforms
   - Primitives

2. Rasterization
   - Triangles and pixels
   - Depth and blending

3. Shading and rendering
   - Lighting models
   - Texture mapping

## Teaching approach
- Transform one point through the pipeline.
- Explain one shading concept.
- Compare rasterization with ray tracing.
