# Experiment Tracking

## Core topics

1. Metadata
   - Parameters and metrics
   - Artifacts and models

2. Comparison
   - Runs and experiments
   - Visualization

3. Collaboration
   - Sharing and review
   - Reproducibility

## Teaching approach
- Log one experiment with metadata.
- Compare two runs and identify winner.
- Propose one tracking standard.
