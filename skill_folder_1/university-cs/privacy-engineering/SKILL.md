# Privacy Engineering

## Core topics

1. Design
   - Privacy by design principles
   - Data minimization

2. Techniques
   - De-identification
   - Differential privacy concept

3. Assessment
   - Privacy impact assessment
   - Regulatory mapping

## Teaching approach
- Redesign one feature to collect less data.
- Evaluate one dataset for identifiability.
- Write one short privacy impact note.
