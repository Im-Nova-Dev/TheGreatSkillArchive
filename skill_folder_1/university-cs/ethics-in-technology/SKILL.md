# Ethics in Technology

## Core topics

1. Social impact
   - Bias and fairness
   - Access and inclusion

2. Safety
   - Responsible design
   - Risk communication

3. Governance
   - Transparency
   - Accountability mechanisms

## Teaching approach
- Review one system for ethical risks.
- Recommend one mitigation.
- Facilitate one structured ethics discussion.
