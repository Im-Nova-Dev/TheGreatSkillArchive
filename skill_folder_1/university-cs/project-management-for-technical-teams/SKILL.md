# Project Management for Technical Teams

## Core topics

1. Planning
   - Scope and milestones
   - Work breakdown structure

2. Estimation
   - Task sizing
   - Uncertainty buffers

3. Delivery
   - Tracking and reporting
   - Risk mitigation

## Teaching approach
- Plan one small technical project.
- Estimate tasks and compare to actuals.
- Write one status update for stakeholders.
