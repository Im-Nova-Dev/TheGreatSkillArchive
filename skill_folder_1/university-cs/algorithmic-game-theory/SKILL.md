# Algorithmic Game Theory

## Core topics

1. Games and equilibrium
   - Normal-form games
   - Nash equilibrium concept

2. Markets and auctions
   - Mechanism design
   - Bidding and revenue

3. Computation
   - Complexity of finding equilibria
   - Hardness

## Teaching approach
- Solve one small normal-form game.
- Compare auction formats.
- Explain one hardness result.
