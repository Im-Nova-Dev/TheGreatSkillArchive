# Healthcare Systems

## Core topics

1. Data and records
   - Electronic health records
   - Interoperability standards

2. Integration
   - Systems and workflows
   - FHIR and HL7 concepts

3. Practice
   - Clinical decision support
   - Privacy and security

## Teaching approach
- Map one hospital IT workflow.
- Evaluate one interoperability need.
- Review one privacy requirement.
