# Quantum Computing Basics

## Core topics

1. Foundations
   - Qubits and measurement
   - Superposition

2. Circuits
   - Gates
   - Simple circuits

3. Algorithms
   - Grover and Deutsch-Jozsa
   - Error correction idea

## Teaching approach
- Represent one simple qubit state.
- Trace one two-qubit circuit step.
- Compare classical versus quantum search idea.
