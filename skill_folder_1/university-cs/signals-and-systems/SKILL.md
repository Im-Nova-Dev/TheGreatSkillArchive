# Signals and Systems

## Core topics

1. Signals
   - Continuous and discrete time
   - Energy and power signals

2. Systems
   - LTI intuition
   - Impulse response and convolution

3. Frequency domain
   - Fourier series and transform
   - Sampling theorem

4. Transforms
   - Laplace transform concept
   - Z-transform concept

## Teaching approach
- Use one audio or communication example.
- Visualize input and output waveforms.
- Explain filter behavior in time and frequency.
