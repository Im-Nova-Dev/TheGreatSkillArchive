# Computational Finance

## Core topics

1. Pricing and models
   - Option pricing basics
   - Pricing methods

2. Risk
   - Portfolio risk
   - Value at risk concept

3. Practice
   - Data and execution
   - Model validation

## Teaching approach
- Model one simple pricing scenario.
- Compare one risk metric choice.
- Critique one backtesting assumption.
