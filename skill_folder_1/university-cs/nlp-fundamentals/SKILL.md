# NLP Fundamentals

## Core topics

1. Text processing
   - Cleaning and tokenization
   - Stopwords and stemming

2. Representation
   - Bag of words
   - Word embeddings

3. Models
   - N-gram and Markov models
   - Neural language models

## Teaching approach
- Process one text corpus end to end.
- Train one simple text classifier.
- Inspect one model's predictions.
