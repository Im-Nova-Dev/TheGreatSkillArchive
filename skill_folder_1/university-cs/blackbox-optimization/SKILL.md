# Blackbox Optimization

## Core topics

1. Methods
   - Gradient-free search
   - Model-based optimization

2. Surrogates
   - Surrogate models
   - Acquisition functions

3. Practice
   - Hyperparameter tuning
   - Budget management

## Teaching approach
- Choose one optimizer for one task.
- Compare random search versus Bayesian optimization.
- Explain one surrogate model role.
