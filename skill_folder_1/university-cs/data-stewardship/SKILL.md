# Data Stewardship

## Core topics

1. Ownership
   - Roles and responsibilities
   - Accountability

2. Quality
   - Standards and metrics
   - Issue management

3. Governance
   - Policy communication
   - Review process

## Teaching approach
- Draft one ownership charter.
- Review one dataset for curation needs.
- Plan one quality improvement.
