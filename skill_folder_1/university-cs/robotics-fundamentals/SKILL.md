# Robotics Fundamentals

## Core topics

1. Kinematics and dynamics
   - Joint and task space
   - Motion basics

2. Sensing and acting
   - Sensors and actuators
   - Feedback control

3. Planning
   - Motion planning idea
   - SLAM concept

## Teaching approach
- Model one arm kinematics simply.
- Compare teleoperation versus autonomous control.
- Discuss one sensing limitation.
