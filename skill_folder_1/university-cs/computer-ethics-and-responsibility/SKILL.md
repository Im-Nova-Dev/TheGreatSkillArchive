# Computer Ethics and Responsibility

## Core topics

1. Ethics concepts
   - Privacy and fairness
   - Accountability and bias

2. Professional responsibility
   - Codes of conduct
   - Disclosure and duty

3. Decision making
   - Stakeholder effects
   - Case-based reasoning

## Teaching approach
- Evaluate one system design for ethical issues.
- Rewrite one feature plan to reduce ethical risk.
- Discuss one realistic dilemma.
