# History of Computing

## Core topics

1. Early computing
   - Mechanical systems
   - Electronic transition

2. Software evolution
   - Languages
   - Operating systems

3. Modern context
   - Internet and mobile
   - AI evolution

## Teaching approach
- Trace one technology lineage.
- Compare one era to modern practice.
- Explain one historical design inertia.
