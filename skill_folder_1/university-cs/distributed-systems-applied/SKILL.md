# Applied Distributed Systems

## Core topics

1. Data patterns
   - Replication and sharding
   - Consistency versus availability

2. Transaction patterns
   - Sagas and compensation
   - Idempotency and ordering

3. Communication
   - Events and queues
   - Request-reply versus fire and forget

## Teaching approach
- Decompose one service into three services.
- Show where failure or delay happens.
- Introduce one pattern per design critique.
