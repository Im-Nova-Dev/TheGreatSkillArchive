# Risk Management

## Core topics

1. Identification
   - Risk sources
   - Probability

2. Assessment
   - Impact analysis
   - Prioritization

3. Response
   - Mitigation
   - Contingency

## Teaching approach
- List risks for one project.
- Evaluate one risk matrix.
- Design one mitigation plan.
