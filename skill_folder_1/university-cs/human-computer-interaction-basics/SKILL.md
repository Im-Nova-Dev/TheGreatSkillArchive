# Human-Computer Interaction Basics

## Core topics

1. Usability
   - Learnability
   - Efficiency and error tolerance
   - Satisfaction

2. Design principles
   - Affordances and signifiers
   - Feedback and visibility
   - Consistency and standards

3. Accessibility
   - Perceptual and motor needs
   - Screen readers and contrast

4. Evaluation
   - Heuristic review
   - Think-aloud testing

## Teaching approach
- Review one interface per session.
- Identify three violations and three improvements.
- Show before and after sketches.
