# Telecommunications

## Core topics

1. Transmission
   - Modulation
   - Multiplexing

2. Networks
   - Protocols and routing
   - Quality of service

3. Systems
   - Cellular and satellite
   - Access and capacity

## Teaching approach
- Compare one modulation scheme.
- Review one network design diagram.
- Explain one latency-versus-reliability choice.
