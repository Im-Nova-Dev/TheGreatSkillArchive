# Mathematical Thinking

## Core topics

1. Rigor
   - Definitions and precision
   - Proof standards

2. Problem solving
   - Example-driven method
   - Generalization

3. Communication
   - Writing proofs
   - Explaining reasoning

## Teaching approach
- Reformulate one informal claim precisely.
- Write one short proof.
- Critique one explanation for rigor gaps.
