# Generative AI Ethics

## Core topics

1. Risks
   - Bias and harm
   - Copyright and provenance

2. Governance
   - Disclosure and labeling
   - Review process

3. Practice
   - Impact measurement
   - Policy and legal

## Teaching approach
- Audit one model use case for ethical risk.
- Evaluate one dataset for provenance gaps.
- Define one responsible deployment rule.
