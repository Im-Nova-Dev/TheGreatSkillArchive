## Key External Approval Models and Workflow Models to Verify From

1. FIX Protocol (FIX Trading Community)
   - Applicable to broker message formats and state transitions.

2. MiFID II / MiFIR
   - Applicable to best execution reporting requirements in Europe.

3. SEC Rule 3110 / 3260
   - Applicable to suitability and supervision requirements.

4. CFTC / NFA Part 4 and 5
   - Applicable to FCM approval and reporting requirements.

5. ISO 20022 and SWIFT workflows
   - Applicable to cross-border payment and settlement messaging.

6. Internal broker document checklist
   - Always verify current broker terms of service for automation.
