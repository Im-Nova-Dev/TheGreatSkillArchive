# Cohen-Lenstra Heuristics Breakthroughs (2024–2026)

Teach the three major recent advances in Cohen-Lenstra heuristics as surveyed in Ellenberg's Séminaire Bourbaki talk #1251 (March 2026, arXiv:2606.06024):
1. **Generalized Cohen-Lenstra-Martinet conjectures** with geometric/function-field proofs (Liu-Wood-Zureick-Brown et al.)
2. **2-primary Cohen-Lenstra for class groups & Selmer groups** in quadratic twist families (Smith 2026) — resolves the minimalist conjecture for elliptic curves
3. **Stevenhagen's conjecture on the negative Pell equation** (Koymans-Pagano 2025)

Covers limiting distributions, moment methods, Selmer group statistics, and the topology-function field bridge.

Use when teaching arithmetic statistics, algebraic number theory, class groups, elliptic curves, or the method of moments in probabilistic number theory.
