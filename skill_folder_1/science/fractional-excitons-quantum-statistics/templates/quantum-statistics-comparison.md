## Key Teaching Points

1. **Three-class paradigm is incomplete** — fractional excitons prove a 4th category exists
2. **Neutral ≠ simple boson** — composite of anyons ≠ sum of their statistics
3. **Experimental handle** — gate voltage tunes filling factor → tunes constituent anyon density → tunes statistics
4. **Majorana parallel** — like Majorana zero modes, fractional excitons offer neutral topological carriers, but in a different platform (FQHE vs. topological superconductors)
